\(K \simeq 4.5\)

3.¬∂

Estimez cette valeur en exploitant les relations et/ou abaques :

On a:

\[ G_{BO}(s)=\frac{K}{(1+s)(1+2s)}=\frac{K}{2s^2+3s+1} \Rightarrow G_{BF}(s)=\frac{K}{2s^2+3s+1+K}=\frac{\frac{K}{K+1}}{\frac{2}{K+1}s^2+\frac{3}{K+1}s+1} \]

On obtient donc par identification:

\[\begin{split} \left\{ \begin{array}{ll} \frac{2\zeta}{\omega_n}=\frac{3}{K+1}\\ \frac{1}{\omega_n^2}=\frac{2}{K+1} \end{array} \right. \end{split}\]

Or via l‚Äô√©quation du d√©passement (cf. p.3-11), on peut d√©duire \(\zeta\):

\[ D_P=100*e^{-\frac{k\pi\zeta}{\sqrt{1-\zeta^2}}} \Rightarrow 20=100*e^{-\frac{\pi\zeta}{\sqrt{1-\zeta^2}}} \Rightarrow \zeta=0.456 \]

D√®s lors, on obtient:

\[\begin{split} \begin{alignat*}{2} \left\{ \begin{aligned} \begin{array}{ll} \frac{3}{K+1}=\frac{0.9}{\omega_n} \\ \frac{1}{\omega_n^2}=\frac{2}{K+1} \end{array} \end{aligned}\right. \Rightarrow \left\{ \begin{aligned} \begin{array}{ll} \mathbf{K=4.4} \\ \omega_n=1.66 \end{array} \end{aligned}\right. \end{alignat*}\ \end{split}\]
